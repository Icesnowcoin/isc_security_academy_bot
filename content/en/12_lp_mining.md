# LP Liquidity Mining: Become Your Own Bank

Liquidity Providers (LPs) are the engine of a DEX. By providing funds to the ISC/BNB pool, you can earn a share of the trading fees generated by the platform.

### Core Concepts
When you provide liquidity, you deposit an equal value of two tokens into a **Liquidity Pair**. In return, you receive **LP Tokens** as a receipt representing your share of the pool. However, you must be aware of **Impermanent Loss**, which occurs when the price ratio of the tokens changes significantly compared to when you deposited them.

### ISC's Liquidity Guarantee
To build community trust, ISC's primary liquidity is locked via **UNCX Locker**. This ensures that no one can suddenly withdraw the liquidity pool, providing a strong safeguard against "Rug Pulls."
