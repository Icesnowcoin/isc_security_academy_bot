# Community Building: The Lifeblood of Decentralization

At ISC, there are no "bosses." Every token holder is a co-builder of the community. The success of a decentralized project depends not on a single person, but on the collective strength of its members.

### Core Principles of Co-Building
The community operates on **collective decision-making**, where every member can influence the project's future and treasury allocation through Snapshot voting. We encourage **collective contribution**, where members leverage their unique skills in development, marketing, or security. This leads to **shared benefits**, as the value created by the project's growth is shared by all co-builders. Finally, we uphold **collective responsibility**, standing united to maintain ecosystem stability in the face of challenges.

Decentralization means the delegation of power and the return of responsibility. At ISC, your voice and actions truly matter.
