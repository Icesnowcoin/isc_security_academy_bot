# Snapshot: Off-Chain Voting for On-Chain Governance

Snapshot is the industry-standard platform for decentralized voting. It allows ISC holders to participate in governance without paying expensive gas fees.

### How It Works
Snapshot uses **off-chain signatures** via IPFS to record votes. Your voting power is determined by the amount of ISC you hold in your wallet at a specific block (the "snapshot"). This ensures that governance is both **cost-effective** and **tamper-proof**, as all results are stored permanently on decentralized storage.

### ISC Community Governance
ISC uses Snapshot for all major community decisions, including treasury expenditures, protocol upgrades, and strategic partnerships. By participating in Snapshot, you directly influence the future direction of the ISC ecosystem.
