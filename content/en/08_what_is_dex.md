# What is a DEX: You Are in Control

Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs) are the cornerstone of the DeFi ecosystem. Unlike traditional Centralized Exchanges (CEXs), DEXs allow users to trade directly on-chain without intermediaries.

### Core Features of a DEX
DEXs are **non-custodial**, meaning assets stay in your wallet and trades are executed by smart contracts. There is **no permission required**; anyone with a wallet and gas fees can trade without KYC. Furthermore, every transaction and every bit of liquidity is **transparent** and verifiable on the blockchain.

### How It Works
Most DEXs use an **Automated Market Maker (AMM)** model, which uses liquidity pools instead of traditional order books. This means you are trading against a smart contract rather than being matched directly with another user.
