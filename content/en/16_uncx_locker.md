# UNCX Locker: Securing ISC Liquidity

UNCX Network (formerly UniCrypt) is a decentralized services provider that ISC uses to lock its liquidity pool (LP) tokens.

### The Importance of LP Locking
Locking liquidity is a critical security measure that **prevents rug pulls**. When LP tokens are locked in a smart contract like UNCX, the developers cannot withdraw the liquidity from the exchange for a specified period. This creates a **transparent trust layer**, as anyone can verify the lock status and duration on the UNCX website.

### ISC's Commitment
By locking our primary liquidity on UNCX, ISC ensures that the market remains stable and that community members can trade with confidence, knowing that the "exit door" is securely locked for the long term.
