# Rewards and Rights: Rewarding Every Contribution

In the ISC decentralized ecosystem, we ensure that contributors receive the rewards they deserve through clear incentive mechanisms. This is not just about material gain, but also about recognizing individual influence and community status.

### Incentive System Overview

| Reward Type | How to Earn | Core Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Token Rewards** | Participate in dev, report major bugs, or marketing | Earn ISC tokens and share project growth |
| **Governance Weight** | Long-term holding, active voting, high-quality proposals | Greater influence over treasury allocations |
| **Honorary Status** | Continuous contribution, volunteering, expert roles | Special Discord roles or community titles |
| **Exit Rights** | Exchange assets via DEX at any time | Ensures individual choice and asset liquidity |

### Evaluation Principles
The community adheres to **transparent evaluation** and **results-oriented** rewards. All incentives are distributed after community public notice or multisig confirmation. We encourage long-term commitment, providing higher levels of authority and resource support to those who consistently create value for the community.
