# Member Duties: Finding Your Role in the ISC Community

A healthy community requires members to play their parts effectively. Based on the level of involvement, ISC members can take on various roles.

### Roles and Responsibilities
**Basic Participants** focus on learning and sharing. They attend the Security Academy, enhance their own prevention awareness, and help spread security knowledge to others. They are also expected to be rational holders who understand the project's vision. **Advanced Contributors** take a more active role by participating in Snapshot governance, creating educational content, or acting as security guardians by alerting the community to potential risks. **Core Developers and Experts** contribute to technical iterations, maintain open-source code, and provide professional strategic analysis for the community's development.

Our code of conduct is simple: respect others, communicate transparently, reject fraud, and always prioritize the collective interest of the community.
