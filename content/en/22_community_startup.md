# Community Startup: Realizing Your Ideas on the ISC Platform

ISC is more than just a token project; it is a decentralized startup incubator. If you have an idea based on security, governance, or community building, the ISC treasury will be your strongest supporter.

### Startup Support Process
1. **Funding Application**: Submit a detailed project proposal to Snapshot, explaining the core idea, execution plan, and required budget.
2. **Traffic Support**: Gain initial users and exposure through ISC's official channels and community groups.
3. **Technical Sharing**: Technical experts within the community provide consulting services such as code audits and architectural design.
4. **Mentor Network**: Connect with industry veterans who provide direction and guidance for your startup project.

### Success Criteria
We value projects that bring real value to the ISC ecosystem, whether by increasing the user base, enhancing security, or improving governance efficiency. Here, there is no bureaucracy—only respect for innovation.
