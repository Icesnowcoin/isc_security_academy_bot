# PancakeSwap: The Liquidity Heart of the BSC Ecosystem

PancakeSwap is the largest decentralized exchange on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) and serves as the primary liquidity hub for the ISC token.

### Why PancakeSwap?
Trading ISC on PancakeSwap offers **minimal fees** compared to Ethereum, as gas costs on BSC are almost negligible. The platform provides **rapid confirmation** with block times of about 3 seconds, ensuring a smooth trading experience. Additionally, it features a **rich ecosystem** beyond simple swapping, including yield farming, IFOs, and lotteries.

### How to Trade ISC on PancakeSwap
First, ensure you have **BNB** in your wallet for gas fees. On the "Swap" page, click the token list and manually paste the ISC contract address. Finally, set an appropriate **Slippage** percentage to ensure your transaction completes successfully.
