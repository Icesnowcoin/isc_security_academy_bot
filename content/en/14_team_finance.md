# Team Finance: Professional Token Vesting for ISC

Team Finance is a leading security platform used by ISC to manage token vesting and linear releases, ensuring transparency for the community.

### Core Functions
The platform provides **token locking and vesting** services, allowing projects to set a clear schedule for when team or treasury tokens become available. This prevents sudden large-scale sell-offs and demonstrates a long-term commitment from the project.

### ISC Use Case
ISC utilizes Team Finance to manage its treasury and team allocations. By using a reputable third-party locker, the community can verify on-chain that tokens are being released according to the roadmap, significantly reducing the risk of a "rug pull" or developer dump.
