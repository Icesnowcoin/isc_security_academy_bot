# What is Decentralization: From Architecture to Governance

Decentralization is the soul of blockchain. It is more than just a technical method; it represents a fundamental redistribution of power and trust.

### Three Dimensions of Decentralization
- **Architectural**: The network consists of thousands of nodes globally with no central server, making it impossible for a single entity to shut it down.
- **Political**: No single individual or organization has total control; decisions are driven by community consensus.
- **Logical**: While the system acts as a single unit, its state is maintained collectively by all participants.

### ISC's Decentralized Practice
ISC has renounced contract ownership, its treasury is managed by community multisig, and all major proposals are decided via Snapshot voting. This design ensures the project does not depend on any "founder" but truly belongs to its holders.
