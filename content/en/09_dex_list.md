# Recommended DEXs: Reliable Choices Across Chains

In a sea of platforms, choosing a DEX with a long track record and comprehensive audit reports is the first step toward secure trading.

### Leading DEXs by Ecosystem
- **PancakeSwap (BSC)**: The primary battlefield for ISC. It offers low fees and high speed, making it the undisputed leader on the BNB Smart Chain.
- **Uniswap (Ethereum/Multi-chain)**: The pioneer of DEXs, featuring the deepest liquidity and the widest support for various tokens.
- **Curve (Stablecoins)**: Optimized for stablecoin trading with extremely low slippage, ideal for large-volume swaps.
- **1inch (Aggregator)**: Not an exchange itself, but an aggregator that automatically finds the best prices across multiple DEXs for you.

### Trading Tip
Regardless of the DEX you use, always enter through official links and double-check the contract address of the token you intend to trade.
